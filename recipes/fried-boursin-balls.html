Fried Boursin Balls

Description

Another recipe take from All Recipes. With herbs in the center, these balls pack a mean crunch!

Ingredients

  • 1 quart canola oil
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2 packages of Boursin Garlic and Fine Herbs Cheese Bites

Steps

  1. Heat oil.
  2. Fill three bowls with flour, eggs and breadcrumbs, respectively.
  3. Dredge cheese in flour and dip in eggs, then cover with breadcrumbs.
  4. Freeze for 15 mintues.
  5. Fry boursin bites
