Black-Eyed Pea Soup

Description

According to All Recipes this is a great dish for cold winter nights and should be enjoyed with corn bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk pork sausage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cups of water
  • 3 cans of black-eyed peas
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of chopped green chilies
  • 4 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • salt, pepper, cumin

Steps

  1. Heat oven.
  2. Cook and stir pork sausage, ground beef and onion for 12 minutes.
  3. Pour in water and stir in rest of ingredients.
  4. Season with spices, salt and pepper.
  5. Boil for 45 minutes.
